What is a power semiconductor device?
Power semiconductor devices are used for switching and rectifying power in electrical systems.
What are power semiconductors used for?
Power semiconductors are used for controlling and converting electrical energy efficiently.
What is the classification of power semiconductor devices?
Power semiconductors are classified into diodes, thyristors, MOSFETs, and IGBTs.
